WooCommerce USPS, UPS & FedEx Shipping Method Plugin uses EasyPost API to Display Live Shipping Rates from USPS, UPS & FedEx based on Shipping Address & Cart Items.

== Description ==
WooCommerce EasyPost USPS, UPS and FedEx Shipping extension with Postage helps WooCommerce shops to streamline EasyPost shipping integration by providing real-time shipping rates. Based on the postal codes and other parameters, all available shipping services along with the rates are listed for the customers to choose from.

= Integrates WooCommerce to USPS, UPS & FedEx using EasyPost API =

Once this plugin is installed and configured with necessary information (please visit installation section for more info), your WordPress/WooCommerce Shop will be ready to ship using USPS, UPS & FedEx using EasyPost API. This plugin will add EasyPost shipping method as one of the shipping methods in WooCommerce.

= Calculate shipping rates dynamically =

While checking out, a customer is presented with the available shipping services and the rates of USPS, UPS & FedEx based on the shipping address and cart content. Customer can choose the best method that matches his/her requirements and proceed to payment.

= About FedEx =
FedEx Corporation is an American multinational courier delivery services company headquartered in Memphis, Tennessee. The company is known for its overnight shipping service, but also for pioneering a system that could track packages and provide real-time updates on package location. FedEx services includes FedEx Express, FedEx Ground, FedEx Freight, etc. FedEx acquired their rival firm TNT Express.

= About UPS =
United Parcel Service (UPS) is a package delivery company and a provider of supply chain management solutions. The UPS global logistics company is headquartered in the U.S. UPS services includes UPS Next Day Air, UPS Ground, UPS Standard, UPS Freight LTL, etc.

= About USPS =
The United States Postal Service (USPS, U.S. Mail, or Postal Service) is an independent agency of the United States federal government responsible for providing postal service in the United States, including its insular areas and associated states. It is one of the few government agencies explicitly authorized by the United States Constitution. 

= About EasyPost =
EasyPost is an American software company that helps businesses integrate shipping from the major carriers. EasyPost was founded in 2012 and is headquartered in San Francisco, California. EasyPost has thirty employees, and ships millions of shipments each month through its API. Developers can integrate the EasyPost API instead of integrating the carriers' SOAP / XML APIs. EasyPost allows business to integrate tracking statuses, address verification, label purchasing, rating, and more from FedEx, UPS, USPS, and other regional carriers into their site or application.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin folder to the ‘/wp-content/plugins/’ directory.

2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress.

3. Thats it – you can now configure the plugin.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= After the setup, my site is still saying “There doesn‘t seem to be any available shipping methods”. =

You can enable debug in settings and debug messages will start showing up in cart/checkout page, from which you will get a clue what is the issue about. Hope EasyPost shipping method is enabled. Product dimensions and weight is configured correctly.
